Notes:
Celebrating our 28th season, Ring of Fire is North Carolina’s premier open club ultimate team. Founded in 1989, Ring is one of the oldest club ultimate teams in the world and has a long-standing tradition of success on the national scene, qualifying for the Club Championships every year except 1 over the last 24, including the last 20 years consecutively.
